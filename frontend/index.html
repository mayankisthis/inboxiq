What is InboxIQ?

Email intelligence without leaving Gmail

InboxIQ is a smart email assistant built for people who live in their inbox. It connects to your Gmail account, analyzes incoming messages with machine learning, and gives you actionable insights — so you spend less time sorting and more time doing.

✉

Works with Gmail

InboxIQ layers on top of your existing Gmail workflow. No migration, no new inbox — just a smarter way to manage what you already have.

⚡

Smart classification

Machine learning automatically categorizes emails by urgency, type, and intent, helping you focus on what needs attention first.

📊

Actionable insights

Get a clear dashboard of your email patterns, priorities, and trends — turning inbox noise into useful information.
